# Contact Page:

Preconditions:
1. Navigate to url 'www.rossmann.pl/kontakt'


TestCase 1 - Validation of Email, Phone Number, and Message Field

    1. Verify that the following fields are marked as required:
		- Email 
		- Message
		- Captcha
    2. Check the validation for each field:
    	- Email (requires '@' symbol)
        - Phone number (only digits are allowed)
        - Message (validate minimum and maximum length)


TestCase 2 - Filling out the Contact Form

    1. Enter correct values into all fields of the contact form.
    2. Submit the form.
    3. Verify that only the captcha validation message is displayed.
