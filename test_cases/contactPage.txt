# ContactPageTest:

Preconditions:
1. Navigate to url 'www.rossmann.pl/kontakt'


TestCase 1 - Sending blank contact form
emptyFieldsValidationMessages()
	
	1. Send the contact form without filling any fields.
	2. Verify the appearance of email, message, and captcha validation messages.


TestCase 2 - Phone number field validation
phoneNumberFieldValidation()

	1. Check the validation of the Phone Number field for:
		- letters
		- special characters
		- Length
		
		
TestCase 3 - Email field validation
emailFieldValidation()

	1. Check the validation of the Email field for:
		- Presence of '@' symbol
		
		
TestCase 4 - Message field validation
messageFieldLengthValidation()

	1. Check the validation of the Message field for:
		- Too short value (<20 char)
		- Too long value (>5 000char)
		of Message field


TestCase 5 - Filling out the Contact Form
captchaValidationMessageWhenOtherFieldsValid()

    1. Enter correct values into all fields of the contact form.
    2. Submit the form.
    3. Verify that only the captcha validation message is displayed.
